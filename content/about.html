About Me

A former financial auditor who has taken a leap of faith into tech, I am passionate about computing resources as a vehicle to revolutionize everyday life and optimize efficiency. I seek to produce creative solutions to practical problems, especially for marginalized groups, through my continuous curiosity and development of my programming skills. Ultimately, I believe that technology has the potential to equalize the playing field and alter lives, and I dream of contributing to this greater purpose.
