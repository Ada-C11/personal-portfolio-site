Origin Story

When I was working in Japan, my boss came up to me and said, "The European division wants us to make a sidebar for their website. Can you do that?

"Of course, sir!" I said.

What that actually meant: "I've never coded a day in my life...but I can certainly try!"

As I worked, a million questions popped up -- What was a php_include statement? How the heck does CSS work? But thanks to the power of Google-sensei (and old-fashioned trial and error!) our company ended up with a working sidebar.

The European division later called and thanked me for my work. But, really...it didn't feel like work at all.

You see, writing code sent me into "the zone"...I blink, and hours disappear in a flash! Excitedly, I began helping my department with other web projects.

Now I'm a student at Ada Developer's Academy. (Note: It took me four tries to get in...but that's another story!)

Dan and Devin are incredible instructors...and I'm thrilled to learn more every day.
